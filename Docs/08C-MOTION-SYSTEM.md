# EcoLoop Motion System

## Philosophy

Motion communicates state.

Motion is never decorative.

---

## Allowed Motion

* spring motion
* parallax
* depth shifts
* particle flow
* magnetic hover

---

## Forbidden Motion

* bounce
* shake
* flashy transitions
* random floating

---

## Durations

Fast:
150ms

Normal:
250ms

Large:
500ms

Hero:
1000ms

---

## Scroll Experience

Sections should reveal progressively.

Avoid AOS-style animations.

Use depth transitions.

---

## Completion Animation

When an action is completed:

Particles collapse inward.

Then reform.

Duration:

800ms
