# EcoLoop — Branding Guidelines

Version: 1.0

Status: Final

Priority: High

---

# Brand Positioning

EcoLoop is not:

* an environmental NGO
* a climate campaign
* a carbon calculator

EcoLoop is:

```txt id="br01"
Personal Environmental Intelligence
```

---

# Brand Personality

Primary Traits:

* intelligent
* calm
* optimistic
* futuristic
* trustworthy

---

Avoid:

* preachy
* activist
* corporate
* childish

---

# Tone of Voice

Good:

```txt id="br02"
Here's your biggest opportunity today.
```

---

Bad:

```txt id="br03"
You are harming the planet.
```

---

# Communication Style

Always:

* informative
* encouraging
* concise

Never:

* guilt-trip
* shame
* exaggerate

---

# Visual Personality

Feels like:

* Apple
* Linear
* Arc
* Notion

---

Not:

* Greenpeace
* WWF
* school sustainability projects

---

# Logo Direction

Logo should represent:

```txt id="br04"
flow
connection
systems
```

---

Not:

* leaves
* trees
* globes
* recycling icons

---

# Iconography

Preferred:

Lucide Icons

Characteristics:

* geometric
* minimal
* consistent

---

# Language Rules

Prefer:

```txt id="br05"
opportunity
improvement
progress
```

---

Avoid:

```txt id="br06"
damage
failure
pollution
guilt
```

---

# Tagline Direction

Possible examples:

```txt id="br07"
See What Your Carbon Can't Hide
```

```txt id="br08"
Understand. Track. Reduce.
```

```txt id="br09"
Environmental Intelligence For Everyday Life
```

---

# Emotional Outcome

Users should leave feeling:

```txt id="br10"
I understand my impact.
```

Not:

```txt id="br11"
I am a bad person.
```

---

# Definition of Done

Every page should feel like it belongs to:

```txt id="br12"
a premium software company
```

not an environmental campaign.

End of Document.
