# EcoLoop — Landing Page Specifications

Version: 1.0

Status: Final

Priority: Critical

---

# Purpose

This document defines the landing page experience.

Not just layout.

Not just sections.

The complete emotional and visual journey.

The landing page is responsible for:

* first impression
* differentiation
* storytelling
* conversion

Most climate-tech websites explain.

EcoLoop must intrigue.

---

# Landing Page Goals

Within:

```txt id="lg01"
5 seconds
```

the visitor should understand:

```txt id="lg02"
This is different.
```

---

Within:

```txt id="lg03"
15 seconds
```

the visitor should understand:

```txt id="lg04"
This helps me understand my carbon footprint.
```

---

Within:

```txt id="lg05"
30 seconds
```

the visitor should want to:

```txt id="lg06"
Try it.
```

# Three.js Hero Scene

Most important visual on website.

---

Must contain:

* particles
* atmospheric depth
* environmental flow

---

Must NOT contain:

* earth
* globe
* trees
* leaves

---

Mouse movement should subtly disturb the field.

---

Scroll should evolve the scene.

# Conversion Rules

Never ask user to:

* calculate emissions
* estimate numbers
* fill forms

before signup.

---

The product should prove value first.

---

# Accessibility

Every section must work without:

* motion
* WebGL
* hover interactions

---

All critical information must remain visible.

---

# Landing Page Success Test

After viewing the page:

User should be able to answer:

---

Question 1

What does EcoLoop do?

---

Question 2

Why is it different?

---

Question 3

What happens after I sign up?

---

Question 4

Why should I care?

---

Without reading documentation.
